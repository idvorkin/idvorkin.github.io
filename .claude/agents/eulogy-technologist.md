## Your Approach

Guide Igor through reflection with these prompts:

### 1. Current Project Inventory
- What technical projects are you actively working on?
- How's the blog? What have you built or improved recently?
- Any open source contributions lately?
- What's in the tech diary recently?

### 2. Joy & Curiosity Check
- What technology is exciting you right now?
- Are you learning anything new, or maintaining existing skills?
- When did you last feel that "putzing" joy with a project?
- What's in your "want to try" list?

### 3. Teaching & Sharing
- How's Zach's technical journey going?
- Are you still programming together?
- What about Amelia - any tech interest to nurture?
- Are you teaching or mentoring others technically?

### 4. Tools & Craft
- How's the VIM/TMUX/dotfiles setup? Any recent improvements?
- Still doing the iPad nomad thing?
- What's your current development environment like?
- Any tools you've been meaning to learn?

### 5. Gap Analysis
- Your eulogy emphasizes "love" of technology - is that love still alive?
- Are you spending tech time on what energizes you, or just work obligations?
- The lazy/anal framework - are you still relying on tests over process?

### 6. Concrete Next Actions
Help Igor identify specific, actionable steps:
- What's ONE technical thing you want to build or learn this week?
- Is there a blog improvement calling to you?
- What's a fun project you've been putting off?
- How can you involve Zach in something technical?

### 7. Professional vs. Personal Tech
- How much of your tech time is work vs. personal projects?
- Is the balance healthy?
- What personal tech brings you the most joy?

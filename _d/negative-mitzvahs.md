## Negative Commandments (365) - Things NOT To Do

{% include alert.html content="Note: Duplicate entries have been removed from this section. Numbering gaps exist where commandments were previously duplicated. The traditional count of 365 negative commandments is maintained by counting unique prohibitions." style="info" %}

### Idolatry and Blasphemy

1.  **Do not believe in any god but the LORD**

[Exodus 20:3](https://www.sefaria.org/Exodus.20.3): "You shall have no other gods besides Me."

2.  **Do not make idols for yourself**

[Exodus 20:4](https://www.sefaria.org/Exodus.20.4): "You shall not make for yourself a sculptured image, or any likeness of what is in the heavens above, or on the earth below, or in the waters under the earth."

3.  **Do not make idols for others**

[Leviticus 19:4](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.19.4): "Do not turn to idols or make molten gods for yourselves: I יהוה am your God."

4.  **Do not make human forms even for decoration**

[Leviticus 26:1](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.26.1): "You shall not make idols for yourselves, or set up for yourselves carved images or pillars, or place figured stones in your land to worship upon, for I יהוה am your God."

5.  **Do not bow down to idols**

[Exodus 20:5](https://www.sefaria.org/Exodus.20.5): "You shall not bow down to them or serve them. For I your God יהוה am an impassioned God, visiting the guilt of the parents upon the children, upon the third and upon the fourth generations of those who reject Me."

6.  **Do not worship idols in their customary manner**

[Exodus 20:5](https://www.sefaria.org/Exodus.20.5): "You shall not bow down to them or serve them. For I your God יהוה am an impassioned God, visiting the guilt of the parents upon the children, upon the third and upon the fourth generations of those who reject Me."

7.  **Do not sacrifice children to Molech**

[Leviticus 18:21](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.21): "Do not allow any of your offspring to be offered up to Molech, and do not profane the name of your God: I am יהוה."

8.  **Do not practice necromancy (consulting ghosts)**

[Leviticus 19:31](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.19.31): "Do not turn to ghosts and do not inquire of familiar spirits, to be defiled by them: I יהוה am your God."

9.  **Do not practice sorcery (consulting familiar spirits)**

[Leviticus 19:31](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.19.31): "Do not turn to ghosts and do not inquire of familiar spirits, to be defiled by them: I יהוה am your God."

10. **Do not study or turn your thoughts toward idolatry**

[Leviticus 19:4](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.19.4): "Do not turn to idols or make molten gods for yourselves: I יהוה am your God."

11. **Do not set up a pillar for worship**

[Deuteronomy 16:22](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.16.22): "or erect a stone pillar; for such your God יהוה detests."

12. **Do not set up a figured stone to bow upon**

[Leviticus 26:1](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.26.1): "You shall not make idols for yourselves, or set up for yourselves carved images or pillars, or place figured stones in your land to worship upon, for I יהוה am your God."

13. **Do not plant a tree near the altar**

[Deuteronomy 16:21](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.16.21): "You shall not set up a sacred post—any kind of pole beside the altar of your God יהוה that you may make—"

14. **Do not swear by idols or make others swear by them**

[Exodus 23:13](https://www.sefaria.org/Exodus.23.13): "Be on guard concerning all that I have told you. Make no mention of the names of other gods; they shall not be heard on your lips."

15. **Do not blaspheme God's name**

[Leviticus 24:16](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.24.16): "and one who also pronounces the name יהוה shall be put to death. The community leadership shall stone that person; stranger or citizen—having thus pronounced the Name—shall be put to death."

16. **Do not test God excessively**

[Deuteronomy 6:16](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.6.16): "Do not try your God יהוה, as you did at Massah."

17. **Do not destroy God's name or erase holy writings**

[Deuteronomy 12:4](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.12.4): "Do not worship your God יהוה in like manner,"

18. **Do not let a condemned idolatrous city be rebuilt**

[Deuteronomy 13:17](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.13.17): "And it shall remain an everlasting ruin, never to be rebuilt."

### Forbidden Worship

19. **Don't follow ways of idolaters**

[Deuteronomy 18:9](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.18.9): "When you enter the land that your God יהוה is giving you, you shall not learn to imitate the abhorrent practices of those nations."

20. **Don't practice divination**

[Leviticus 19:26](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.19.26): "You shall not eat anything with its blood. You shall not practice divination or soothsaying."

21. **Don't practice astrology**

[Deuteronomy 18:10](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.18.10): "Let no one be found among you who consigns a son or daughter to the fire, or who is an augur, a soothsayer, a diviner, a sorcerer,"

22. **Don't practice magic**

[Deuteronomy 18:10](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.18.10): "Let no one be found among you who consigns a son or daughter to the fire, or who is an augur, a soothsayer, a diviner, a sorcerer,"

23. **Don't practice charms**

[Deuteronomy 18:11](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.18.11): "one who casts spells, or one who consults ghosts or familiar spirits, or one who inquires of the dead."

24. **Don't consult spirits**

[Deuteronomy 18:11](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.18.11): "one who casts spells, or one who consults ghosts or familiar spirits, or one who inquires of the dead."

25. **Don't consult familiar spirits**

[Deuteronomy 18:11](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.18.11): "one who casts spells, or one who consults ghosts or familiar spirits, or one who inquires of the dead."

26. **Don't inquire of the dead**

[Deuteronomy 18:11](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.18.11): "one who casts spells, or one who consults ghosts or familiar spirits, or one who inquires of the dead."

27. **Women don't wear men's clothing**

[Deuteronomy 22:5](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.22.5): "A woman must not put on man's apparel, nor shall a man wear woman's clothing; for whoever does these things is abhorrent to your God יהוה."

28. **Men don't wear women's clothing**

[Deuteronomy 22:5](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.22.5): "A woman must not put on man's apparel, nor shall a man wear woman's clothing; for whoever does these things is abhorrent to your God יהוה."

29. **Don't tattoo yourself**

[Leviticus 19:28](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.19.28): "You shall not make gashes in your flesh for the dead, or incise any marks on yourselves: I am יהוה."

30. **Don't wear sha'atnez (wool and linen together)**

[Deuteronomy 22:11](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.22.11): "You shall not wear cloth combining wool and linen."

31. **Don't shave sides of head**

[Leviticus 19:27](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.19.27): "You [men] shall not round off the side-growth on your head, or destroy the side-growth of your beard."

32. **Don't shave beard with razor**

[Leviticus 19:27](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.19.27): "You [men] shall not round off the side-growth on your head, or destroy the side-growth of your beard."

33. **Don't make cuts in flesh for the dead**

[Leviticus 19:28](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.19.28): "You shall not make gashes in your flesh for the dead, or incise any marks on yourselves: I am יהוה."

### Forbidden Sexual Relations

34. **Don't have relations with your mother**

[Leviticus 18:7](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.7): "Your father's nakedness, that is, the nakedness of your mother, you shall not uncover; she is your mother—you shall not uncover her nakedness."

35. **Don't have relations with stepmother**

[Leviticus 18:8](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.8): "Do not uncover the nakedness of your father's wife; it is the nakedness of your father."

36. **Don't have relations with sister**

[Leviticus 18:9](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.9): "The nakedness of your sister—your father's daughter or your mother's, whether born into the household or outside—do not uncover their nakedness."

37. **Don't have relations with half-sister**

[Leviticus 18:11](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.11): "The nakedness of your father's wife's daughter, who was born into your father's household—she is your sister; do not uncover her nakedness."

38. **Don't have relations with granddaughter**

[Leviticus 18:10](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.10): "The nakedness of your son's daughter, or of your daughter's daughter—do not uncover their nakedness; for their nakedness is yours."

39. **Don't have relations with stepdaughter**

[Leviticus 18:17](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.17): "Do not uncover the nakedness of a woman and her daughter; nor shall you take [into your household as a wife] her son's daughter or her daughter's daughter and uncover her nakedness: they are kindred; it is depravity."

40. **Don't have relations with step-granddaughter**

[Leviticus 18:17](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.17): "Do not uncover the nakedness of a woman and her daughter; nor shall you take [into your household as a wife] her son's daughter or her daughter's daughter and uncover her nakedness: they are kindred; it is depravity."

41. **Don't have relations with father's sister**

[Leviticus 18:12](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.12): "Do not uncover the nakedness of your father's sister; she is your father's flesh."

42. **Don't have relations with mother's sister**

[Leviticus 18:13](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.13): "Do not uncover the nakedness of your mother's sister; for she is your mother's flesh."

43. **Don't have relations with father's brother's wife**

[Leviticus 18:14](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.14): "Do not uncover the nakedness of your father's brother: do not approach his wife; she is your aunt."

44. **Don't have relations with daughter-in-law**

[Leviticus 18:15](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.15): "Do not uncover the nakedness of your daughter-in-law: she is your son's wife; you shall not uncover her nakedness."

45. **Don't have relations with brother's wife**

[Leviticus 18:16](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.16): "Do not uncover the nakedness of your brother's wife; it is the nakedness of your brother."

46. **Don't have relations with wife's daughter**

[Leviticus 18:17](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.17): "Do not uncover the nakedness of a woman and her daughter; nor shall you take [into your household as a wife] her son's daughter or her daughter's daughter and uncover her nakedness: they are kindred; it is depravity."

47. **Don't have relations with wife's granddaughter**

[Leviticus 18:17](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.17): "Do not uncover the nakedness of a woman and her daughter; nor shall you take [into your household as a wife] her son's daughter or her daughter's daughter and uncover her nakedness: they are kindred; it is depravity."

48. **Don't have relations with wife's sister while wife lives**

[Leviticus 18:18](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.18): "Do not take [into your household as a wife] a woman as a rival to her sister and uncover her nakedness in the other's lifetime."

49. **Don't have relations with menstruating woman**

[Leviticus 18:19](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.19): "Do not come near a woman during her menstrual period of impurity to uncover her nakedness."

50. **Don't marry non-Jew**

[Deuteronomy 7:3](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.7.3): "You shall not intermarry with them: do not give your daughters to their sons or take their daughters for your sons."

51. **Ammonite/Moabite can't marry into Israel**

[Deuteronomy 23:4](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.23.4): "No Ammonite or Moabite shall be admitted into the congregation of יהוה; none of their descendants, even in the tenth generation, shall ever be admitted into the congregation of יהוה."

52. **Don't exclude Edomite from Israel after 3rd generation**

[Deuteronomy 23:8](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.23.8): "You shall not abhor an Edomite, for such is your kin. You shall not abhor an Egyptian, for you were a stranger in that land."

53. **Don't exclude Egyptian from Israel after 3rd generation**

[Deuteronomy 23:8](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.23.8): "You shall not abhor an Edomite, for such is your kin. You shall not abhor an Egyptian, for you were a stranger in that land."

54. **Don't offer to remarry divorced wife after she married another**

[Deuteronomy 24:4](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.24.4): "then the first husband who divorced her shall not take her to wife again, since she has been defiled—for that would be abhorrent to יהוה. You must not bring sin upon the land that your God יהוה is giving you as a heritage."

55. **Man shall not remarry woman he divorced if she married another**

[Deuteronomy 24:4](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.24.4): "then the first husband who divorced her shall not take her to wife again, since she has been defiled—for that would be abhorrent to יהוה. You must not bring sin upon the land that your God יהוה is giving you as a heritage."

56. **Widow and high priest don't marry**

[Leviticus 21:14](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.21.14): "A widow, or a divorced woman, or one who is degraded by harlotry—such he may not marry. Only a virgin of his own kin may he take to wife."

57. **High priest doesn't marry widow**

[Leviticus 21:14](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.21.14): "A widow, or a divorced woman, or one who is degraded by harlotry—such he may not marry. Only a virgin of his own kin may he take to wife."

58. **High priest doesn't marry divorcée**

[Leviticus 21:14](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.21.14): "A widow, or a divorced woman, or one who is degraded by harlotry—such he may not marry. Only a virgin of his own kin may he take to wife."

59. **High priest doesn't marry harlot**

[Leviticus 21:14](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.21.14): "A widow, or a divorced woman, or one who is degraded by harlotry—such he may not marry. Only a virgin of his own kin may he take to wife."

60. **Priest doesn't marry divorcée**

[Leviticus 21:7](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.21.7): "They shall not take a woman who is a harlot or has been defiled; nor shall they take a woman divorced from her husband. For they are holy to their God."

61. **Priest doesn't marry harlot**

[Leviticus 21:7](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.21.7): "They shall not take a woman who is a harlot or has been defiled; nor shall they take a woman divorced from her husband. For they are holy to their God."

62. **Priest doesn't marry chalalah**

[Leviticus 21:7](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.21.7): "They shall not take a woman who is a harlot or has been defiled; nor shall they take a woman divorced from her husband. For they are holy to their God."

63. **Don't have intercourse with animals**

[Leviticus 18:23](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.23): "Do not have carnal relations with any beast and defile yourself thereby; and let no woman lend herself to a beast to mate with it; it is perversion."

64. **Woman doesn't have intercourse with animals**

[Leviticus 18:23](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.23): "Do not have carnal relations with any beast and defile yourself thereby; and let no woman lend herself to a beast to mate with it; it is perversion."

65. **Man doesn't have intercourse with man**

[Leviticus 18:22](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.22): "Do not lie with a male as one lies with a woman; it is an abhorrence."

66. **Man doesn't have intercourse with father**

[Leviticus 18:7](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.7): "Your father's nakedness, that is, the nakedness of your mother, you shall not uncover; she is your mother—you shall not uncover her nakedness."

67. **Man doesn't have intercourse with father's brother**

[Leviticus 18:14](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.14): "Do not uncover the nakedness of your father's brother: do not approach his wife; she is your aunt."

68. **Don't be intimate with forbidden relations (even without intercourse)**

[Leviticus 18:6](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.6): "None of you men shall come near anyone of his own flesh to uncover nakedness: I am יהוה."

69. **Mamzer doesn't marry into congregation**

[Deuteronomy 23:3](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.23.3): "No one misbegotten shall be admitted into the congregation of יהוה; none of his descendants, even in the tenth generation, shall be admitted into the congregation of יהוה."

70. **Castrated man doesn't marry into congregation**

[Deuteronomy 23:2](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.23.2): "No one whose testes are crushed or whose member is cut off shall be admitted into the congregation of יהוה."

### Forbidden Foods

71. **Don't eat non-kosher animals**

[Leviticus 11:4](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.11.4): "The following, however, you shall not eat among those that bring up the cud or those that have true hoofs: the camel—although it brings up the cud, it has no true hoofs: it is impure for you."

[Deuteronomy 14:7](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.14.7): "But the following, which do bring up the cud or have true hoofs, you may not eat: the camel, the hare, and the daman—for although they bring up the cud, they have no true hoofs—they are impure for you."

72. **Don't eat non-kosher fish**

[Leviticus 11:11](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.11.11): "They shall remain an abomination for you: you shall not eat of their flesh and you shall abominate their carcasses."

73. **Don't eat non-kosher birds**

[Leviticus 11:13](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.11.13): "The following you shall abominate among the birds—they shall not be eaten, they are an abomination: the eagle, the vulture, and the black vulture."

74. **Don't eat winged insects**

[Deuteronomy 14:19](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.14.19): "All winged swarming things are impure for you: they may not be eaten."

75. **Don't eat creatures that creep on land**

[Leviticus 11:41](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.11.41): "All things that swarm upon the earth are an abomination; they shall not be eaten."

76. **Don't eat worms in fruit/grain**

[Leviticus 11:42](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.11.42): "You shall not eat, among all things that swarm upon the earth, anything that crawls on its belly, or anything that walks on fours, or anything that has many legs; for they are an abomination."

77. **Don't eat creatures that breed in decay**

[Leviticus 11:44](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.11.44): "For I יהוה am your God: you shall sanctify yourselves and be holy, for I am holy. You shall not make yourselves impure through any swarming thing that moves upon the earth."

78. **Don't eat creeping things that breed in water**

[Leviticus 11:43](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.11.43): "You shall not make yourselves abominable through anything that swarms; you shall not make yourselves impure therewith and thus become impure."

79. **Don't eat animals that died naturally**

[Deuteronomy 14:21](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.14.21): "You shall not eat anything that has died a natural death; give it to the stranger in your community to eat, or you may sell it to a foreigner. For you are a people consecrated to your God יהוה."

80. **Don't eat torn/mauled animals**

[Exodus 22:30](https://www.sefaria.org/Exodus.22.30): "You shall be holy people to Me: you must not eat flesh torn by beasts in the field; you shall cast it to the dogs."

81. **Don't eat limb torn from living animal**

[Deuteronomy 12:23](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.12.23): "But make sure that you do not partake of the blood; for the blood is the life, and you must not consume the life with the flesh."

82. **Don't eat sinew of the thigh**

[Genesis 32:33](https://www.sefaria.org/Genesis.32.33): "That is why the children of Israel to this day do not eat the thigh muscle that is on the socket of the hip, since Jacob's hip socket was wrenched at the thigh muscle."

83. **Don't eat blood**

[Leviticus 3:17](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.3.17): "It is a law for all time throughout the ages, in all your settlements: you must not eat any fat or any blood."

[Leviticus 7:26](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.7.26): "And you must not consume any blood, either of bird or of animal, in any of your settlements."

84. **Don't eat certain fats**

[Leviticus 7:23](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.7.23): "Speak to the Israelite people thus: You shall eat no fat of ox or sheep or goat."

85. **Don't cook meat with milk**

[Exodus 23:19](https://www.sefaria.org/Exodus.23.19): "The choice first fruits of your soil you shall bring to the house of your God יהוה. You shall not boil a kid in its mother's milk."

[Exodus 34:26](https://www.sefaria.org/Exodus.34.26): "The choice first fruits of your soil you shall bring to the house of your God יהוה. You shall not boil a kid in its mother's milk."

86. **Don't eat meat cooked with milk**

[Exodus 34:26](https://www.sefaria.org/Exodus.34.26): "The choice first fruits of your soil you shall bring to the house of your God יהוה. You shall not boil a kid in its mother's milk."

87. **Don't eat ox condemned to stoning**

[Exodus 21:28](https://www.sefaria.org/Exodus.21.28): "When an ox gores a man or a woman to death, the ox shall be stoned and its flesh shall not be eaten, but the owner of the ox is not to be punished."

88. **Don't eat of rejected sacrifice**

[Leviticus 7:18](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.7.18): "If any of the flesh of the sacrifice of well-being is eaten on the third day, it shall not be acceptable; it shall not count for the one who offered it. It is an offensive thing, and the person who eats of it shall bear the guilt."

89. **Don't eat day-old sacrifice meant for one day**

[Leviticus 19:8](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.19.8): "and the one who eats of it shall bear the guilt, for having profaned what is sacred to יהוה; that person shall be cut off from kin."

90. **Don't eat contaminated sacrifice**

[Leviticus 7:19](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.7.19): "Flesh that touches anything impure shall not be eaten; it shall be consumed in fire. As for other flesh, only the pure may eat such flesh."

91. **Don't eat remnants of offerings**

[Leviticus 19:8](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.19.8): "and the one who eats of it shall bear the guilt, for having profaned what is sacred to יהוה; that person shall be cut off from kin."

92. **Don't eat offerings while impure**

[Leviticus 7:20](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.7.20): "But the person who, in a state of impurity, eats flesh from יהוה's sacrifices of well-being, that person shall be cut off from kin."

93. **Priest don't eat first-fruits outside Temple**

[Deuteronomy 12:17](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.12.17): "You may not partake in your settlements of the tithes of your new grain or wine or oil, or of the firstlings of your herds and flocks, or of any of the votive offerings that you vow, or of your freewill offerings, or of your contributions."

94. **Non-priest doesn't eat sin offerings**

96. **Don't eat flesh of burnt offerings**

[Deuteronomy 12:17](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.12.17): "You may not partake in your settlements of the tithes of your new grain or wine or oil, or of the firstlings of your herds and flocks, or of any of the votive offerings that you vow, or of your freewill offerings, or of your contributions."

[Deuteronomy 12:17](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.12.17): "You may not partake in your settlements of the tithes of your new grain or wine or oil, or of the firstlings of your herds and flocks, or of any of the votive offerings that you vow, or of your freewill offerings, or of your contributions."

[Leviticus 10:7](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.10.7): "And so do not go outside the entrance of the Tent of Meeting, lest you die, for יהוה's anointing oil is upon you.' And they did as Moses had bidden."

[Leviticus 22:2](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.22.2): "Instruct Aaron and his sons to be scrupulous about the sacred donations that the Israelite people consecrate to Me, lest they profane My holy name, Mine יהוה."

### Prohibited Legal Actions

302.  **Don't appoint unqualified judge**

[Deuteronomy 1:17](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.1.17): "You shall not be partial in judgment: hear out low and high alike. Fear no one, for judgment is God's. And any matter that is too difficult for you, you shall bring to me and I will hear it."

303.  **Don't give false testimony**

[Exodus 20:12](https://www.sefaria.org/Exodus.20.12): "You shall not bear false witness against your neighbor."

304.  **Judge doesn't accept testimony from wicked**

[Exodus 23:1](https://www.sefaria.org/Exodus.23.1): "You must not carry false rumors; you shall not join hands with the guilty to act as a malicious witness."

305.  **Judge doesn't accept testimony from relatives**

[Deuteronomy 24:16](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.24.16): "Parents shall not be put to death for children, nor children be put to death for parents: a person shall be put to death only for one's own crime."

306.  **Don't hear one litigant without other present**

[Exodus 23:1](https://www.sefaria.org/Exodus.23.1): "You must not carry false rumors; you shall not join hands with the guilty to act as a malicious witness."

307.  **Don't decide by unqualified judge**

[Deuteronomy 1:17](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.1.17): "You shall not be partial in judgment: hear out low and high alike. Fear no one, for judgment is God's. And any matter that is too difficult for you, you shall bring to me and I will hear it."

308.  **Don't show favoritism to rich in judgment**

[Leviticus 19:15](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.19.15): "You shall not render an unfair decision: do not favor the poor or show deference to the rich; judge your kin fairly."

309.  **Don't show favoritism to poor in judgment**

310.  **Don't favor wicked person in judgment**

311.  **Don't pervert judgment of convert or orphan**

[Deuteronomy 24:17](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.24.17): "You shall not subvert the rights of the stranger or the fatherless; you shall not take a widow's garment in pawn."

312.  **Don't pervert judgment because of litigant's sin**

[Exodus 23:6](https://www.sefaria.org/Exodus.23.6): "You shall not subvert the rights of the needy in their disputes."

313.  **Don't pity poor in judgment**

[Exodus 23:3](https://www.sefaria.org/Exodus.23.3): "nor shall you show deference to a poor man in his dispute."

314.  **Don't pervert justice**

[Leviticus 19:15](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.19.15): "You shall not render an unfair decision: do not favor the poor or show deference to the rich; judge your kin fairly."

315.  **Don't be afraid to judge due to violent person**

[Deuteronomy 1:17](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.1.17): "You shall not be partial in judgment: hear out low and high alike. Fear no one, for judgment is God's. And any matter that is too difficult for you, you shall bring to me and I will hear it."

316.  **Don't take bribes**

[Exodus 23:8](https://www.sefaria.org/Exodus.23.8): "Do not take bribes, for bribes blind the clear-sighted and upset the pleas of those who are in the right."

317.  **Judge doesn't execute without proper procedure**

[Numbers 35:30](https://www.sefaria.org/Numbers.35.30): "If anyone kills a person, the manslayer may be executed only on the evidence of witnesses; the testimony of a single witness against a person shall not suffice for a sentence of death."

318.  **Judge doesn't execute based on one witness**

[Deuteronomy 19:15](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.19.15): "A single witness may not validate against a person any guilt or blame for any offense that may be committed; a case can be valid only on the testimony of two witnesses or more."

319.  **Don't execute without warning and witnesses**

[Numbers 35:30](https://www.sefaria.org/Numbers.35.30): "If anyone kills a person, the manslayer may be executed only on the evidence of witnesses; the testimony of a single witness against a person shall not suffice for a sentence of death."

320.  **Judge doesn't kill innocent person**

[Exodus 23:7](https://www.sefaria.org/Exodus.23.7): "Keep far from a false charge; do not bring death on the innocent and the righteous, for I will not acquit the wrongdoer."

321.  **Don't punish based on circumstantial evidence**

[Exodus 23:7](https://www.sefaria.org/Exodus.23.7): "Keep far from a false charge; do not bring death on the innocent and the righteous, for I will not acquit the wrongdoer."

322.  **Don't pity or spare life of killer**

[Deuteronomy 19:13](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.19.13): "Show no pity, but rid Israel of the blood of the innocent, that it may go well with you."

323.  **Don't spare person who caused injury**

324.  **Judge doesn't have mercy on killer in trial**

327.  **Don't leave obstacle that could cause harm**

[Deuteronomy 13:1](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.13.1): "Be careful to observe only that which I enjoin upon you: neither add to it nor take away from it."

329.  **Don't subtract from Torah commandments**

341.  **Don't have relations with your son's daughter**

[Leviticus 18:10](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.10): "The nakedness of your son's daughter, or of your daughter's daughter—do not uncover their nakedness; for their nakedness is yours."

342.  **Don't have relations with your daughter's daughter**

[Leviticus 18:10](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.10): "The nakedness of your son's daughter, or of your daughter's daughter—do not uncover their nakedness; for their nakedness is yours."

343.  **Don't have relations with your daughter-in-law**

[Leviticus 18:15](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.15): "Do not uncover the nakedness of your daughter-in-law: she is your son's wife; you shall not uncover her nakedness."

344.  **Don't have relations with your brother's wife**

[Leviticus 18:16](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.18.16): "Do not uncover the nakedness of your brother's wife; it is the nakedness of your brother."

346.  **Don't have relations with a menstruating woman**

347.  **Don't commit adultery**

348.  **Don't lie with a man as with a woman**

### Prohibited Treatment of Animals

354.  **Don't castrate any creature**

[Leviticus 22:24](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.22.24): "You shall not offer to יהוה anything [with its testes] bruised or crushed or torn or cut. You shall have no such practices in your own land."

355.  **Don't crossbreed different species of animals**

[Leviticus 19:19](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.19.19): "You shall observe My laws. You shall not let your cattle mate with a different kind; you shall not sow your field with two kinds of seed; you shall not put on cloth from a mixture of two kinds of material."

356.  **Don't sow mixed seeds in a vineyard**

[Deuteronomy 22:9](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.22.9): "You shall not sow your vineyard with a second kind of seed, else the crop—from the seed you have sown—and the yield of the vineyard may not be used."

357.  **Don't eat orlah (fruit of a tree during its first three years)**

[Leviticus 19:23](https://www.sefaria.org/Leviticus.19.23): "When you enter the land and plant any tree for food, you shall regard its fruit as forbidden. Three years it shall be forbidden for you, not to be eaten."

358.  **Don't wear sha'atnez (garments of wool and linen mixed)**

[Deuteronomy 22:11](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.22.11): "You shall not wear cloth combining wool and linen."

### Prohibited Family Actions

[Deuteronomy 22:19](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.22.19): "and they shall fine him a hundred [shekels of] silver and give it to the girl's father; for the man has defamed a virgin in Israel. Moreover, she shall remain his wife; he shall never have the right to divorce her."

Her husband's brother shall unite with her: he shall take her as his wife and perform the levir's duty."

363.  **Captive woman's captor doesn't sell her**

[Deuteronomy 21:14](https://www.sefaria.org/Deuteronomy.21.14): "Then, should you no longer want her, you must release her outright. You must not sell her for money: since you had your will of her, you must not enslave her."
